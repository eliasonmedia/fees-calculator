FeeCalc :: About

My name is Michael. Since I started working at Dwolla it became clear to me that payment fees are really confusing. I put this calculator together so people could easily understand how different payment system fees compare.

There are about 2000 different pricing models for different payment systems so the numbers could vary a little bit here or there so you shouldn't use this 100% accurate since you might qualify for some different pricing structure.

If you have any suggestions please reach out on Twitter - and I'll be happy to update this.
